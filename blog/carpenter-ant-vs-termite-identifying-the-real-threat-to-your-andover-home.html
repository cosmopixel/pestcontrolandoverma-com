Key Differences Between Carpenter Ants and Termites

Physical Appearance

  • Carpenter Ants: Black or dark brown, narrow waist, bent antennae
  • Termites: Light-colored, straight body, straight antennae

Damage Patterns

  • Carpenter Ants: Smooth, clean tunnels with sawdust-like material
  • Termites: Rough tunnels filled with soil and excrement

Signs of Infestation in Andover Homes

Carpenter Ant Indicators

  • Wood shavings near wooden structures
  • Large black ants, especially at night
  • Hollow-sounding wood when tapped
  • Visible smooth tunnels in wood

Termite Warning Signs

  • Mud tubes on foundation walls
  • Swarmers (flying termites) in spring
  • Damaged wood that sounds hollow
  • Buckling paint or wallpaper

  • Remove dead trees and stumps near your home
  • Fix moisture issues and leaks promptly
  • Maintain proper ventilation in crawl spaces
  • Store firewood away from your house
