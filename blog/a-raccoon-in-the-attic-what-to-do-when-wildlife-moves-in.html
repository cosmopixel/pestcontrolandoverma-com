Signs You Have a Raccoon in Your Attic

Before taking action, confirm your suspicions by looking for these telltale signs:

  • Heavy thumping or scratching sounds, especially at night
  • Damaged roof vents or fascia boards
  • Dark stains on ceiling or walls from urine
  • Visible raccoon droppings in or around your property
  • Strange chirping or vocal sounds from the attic

Immediate Steps to Take

If you suspect a raccoon invasion in your Andover home, follow these crucial steps:

  1. Don't attempt to remove the raccoon yourself
  2. Document any visible damage for insurance purposes
  3. Keep children and pets away from the affected area
  4. Seal off access to other parts of your home
  5. Contact professional wildlife removal experts immediately

Health and Safety Concerns

Raccoons can pose serious health risks to Andover residents. Similar to other pest issues we address in our home pest control services, raccoon infestations require professional handling due to:

  • Risk of rabies transmission
  • Parasites and bacterial infections
  • Contamination from droppings
  • Structural damage to your home

Professional Removal Process

  4. Cleaning and sanitization

Prevention Tips for Andover Homeowners

After removal, prevent future invasions with these expert tips:

  • Install chimney caps and roof vent covers
  • Trim tree branches away from your roof
  • Secure garbage bins with animal-proof lids
  • Remove outdoor food sources
  • Regular property maintenance and inspection
