Why Rodents Seek Shelter in Winter

When cold weather hits Andover and neighboring communities like Methuen, mice and rats actively seek warm spaces to nest. Your home provides everything they need:

  • Warmth and shelter from harsh winter conditions
  • Access to food and water sources
  • Protected spaces for nesting and breeding
  • Multiple entry points as small as a dime

Common Signs of Rodent Activity

Beyond the telltale scratching sounds, several indicators suggest rodent presence in your home:

  • Droppings near food sources or along walls
  • Gnaw marks on wood, wiring, or food packaging
  • Greasy rub marks along baseboards
  • Unusual pet behavior, especially at night
  • Musty odors in enclosed spaces

Health Risks and Property Damage

Rodent infestations pose serious concerns for Boxford and Andover homeowners. These pests can:

  • Spread diseases through droppings and urine
  • Contaminate food storage areas
  • Chew through electrical wiring, creating fire hazards
  • Damage insulation and structural materials
  • Create entry points for other pests

Prevention Tips for Essex County Homes

While our professional pest control services provide comprehensive solutions, these preventive measures can help:

  • Seal exterior gaps and cracks with appropriate materials
  • Install door sweeps and weather stripping
  • Keep food in sealed containers
  • Remove outdoor wood piles and debris from house foundations
  • Trim tree branches away from rooflines
